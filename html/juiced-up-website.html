Juiced Up is a mobile first website project I created from scratch with Front End HTML, CSS, SASS, JQuery and Javascript.

Juiced Up is a ficticous juicebar branch based in Wellington that provides freshly made juice in glass jars for both take away orders and to drink at the bar. The responsive website allows users to order a juice online through an order-form. Here they simply choose their choice of juice and which of the juice bars they would like to make their juice, depending on their location at the time.

In addition to this main function, I also included a custom Google Maps embedded into the website that shows the users location and the walking distance to each of the bars spread out in Wellington.

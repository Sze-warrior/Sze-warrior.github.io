About Me


I am a Digital Media Designer who focuses on User Interface and User Experience Design. I live to craft, design, research and understand how websites and web applications can help better serve peoples needs and also push me to new heights in my career.

I am currently working as a Junior Designer at Pushpay and have had industry experience before this position too. This includes working as a Information Mangement Design Intern at Inland Revenue Wellington, completing an Interaction Design Intern at New Zealand Post, working for multiple different start-ups in Lightning Lab as a Design Lab Tech, working at Flexitime as a Designer, completing projects for multiple clients at Designgel, working as a Motion Graphics Intern at The Ministry for Primary Industries, as well as competing in an 8 hour Wellington Hackathon and winning the best user-experience award in a student competition.

Through both studying a Bachelor of Design Innovation at Victoria University and working in the industry, I have gained a wide range of skills. This includes Interaction Design, User-Interface Design, User-Experience Design, Web Design, Front End Languages such as HTML, CSS, SASS, LESS, Git and JQuery, Icon Design, Rapid Prototyping, GitHub Mac and more.

I am always open to hearing about upcoming projects both overseas or within New Zealand that will utilize my current skills, challenge me to learn and allow me to contribute proactively to an organisation.
